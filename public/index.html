About ASPO Healthcare

Established in 2014 at SAHA, Ambala (Haryana) as a Contract Research and Manufacturing (CRAMS) organization

ASPO Healthcare Private Ltd. is a premier Pharmaceutical Franchise company in Ambala, boasting an impressive portfolio of 1200+ Brands and Formulations. Under the leadership of Mr. Munish Gupta and Raghav Gupta, we have garnered invaluable expertise in the pharmaceutical industry.

We are known for our WHO-GMP (World Health Organization – Good Manufacturing Practices) and GLP (Good Laboratory Practices) compliant manufacturing units, ensuring high standards of production and quality control in pharmaceuticals.

Our mission is to make essential medicines accessible through trusted supply chains and long-term partnerships, fostering healthcare excellence across India.
